What Drives Me

From an early age, I was always curious how things worked. My first major discovery was electricity, when I realized that the christmas lights all went out after snipping out a single bulb. I've since been less daring, but still on a discovery to find out the inner workings of things in the world. I believe it is this quality that has led me to my success in web development. Things work because I know how it is working underneath. And when things don't? Another bug means another opportunity to learn something new or unexpected!

Why Programming?

My first taste of programming began with an intro to CS in high school. While everyone was working on a simple text based applet for their capstone project, I pushed myself to do something more exciting - Reversi. Class time wasn't enough here, so I stayed after school, worked lunch hours, and set up my own development environment at home. In the end, I realized I could have only accomplished this because of the passion and hard work I put into programming. I've never experienced a place to use creativity and logic in such novel ways, and found it to be profoundly rewarding.
